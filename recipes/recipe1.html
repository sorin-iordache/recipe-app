Pasta with Garlic and Olive Oil (Aglio e Olio)

Ingredients

  • 200 g pasta (spaghetti or any kind you prefer)
  • 3–4 garlic cloves
  • 4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, to taste)
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh parsley (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

👨‍🍳 Simple Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water according to the package instructions. Save a cup of the pasta water before draining.
  2. Slice the garlic thinly
  3. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over low heat.
  4. Add the garlic and, if you like it spicy, the red pepper flakes. Cook gently until the garlic turns golden (don’t let it burn!).
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and mix well.
  6. If needed,pour in a bit of pasta water to help create a light sauce.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  8. Finally, sprinkle with chopped parsley and Parmesan if desired.

✨ Tips:

  • Serve it with a side salad or fresh cherry tomatoes.
  • You can add canned tuna, sautéed mushrooms, or cooked chicken pieces for a heartier version.
