Simple Homemade Vegetable Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 small potato
  • 1 celery stalk (optional)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 500 ml vegetable broth (or water + 1 veggie bouillon cube)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley (optional)
