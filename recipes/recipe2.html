Simple Mediterranean Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 large tomatoes or 6–8 cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 100 g feta cheese (or similar white cheese)
  • 10–12 olives (Kalamata preferred)
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar (red wine or balsamic)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Dried oregano (optional)

👨‍🍳 Simple Steps

  1. Chop the veggies: dice the tomatoes, slice the cucumber, and thinly slice the red onion.
  2. Combine all the vegetables in a large bowl.
  3. Add the olives and top with cubed or crumbled feta cheese.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  6. Toss gently and serve right away.

✨ Tips:

  • You can add extras like avocado, bell pepper, or fresh greens.
  • Serve with toasted bread or warm pita for a complete meal.
